About Me

I grew up farming and ranching in northern utah, and it was during this time that I became plauged with the "farming bug". All I wanted to do was farm, just like my dad and grandfathers had done. However, I also always wanted to be a scientist. When I arrived at Snow College to persue an Associate's of Science degree, I found a real passion for agricultural sciences, plant science in particular. Upon transferring to Utah Valley University, I was very fortunate to land a sppot in Dr. Yu-Ya Liang's research lab. With her help, I have been able to develop and carry out my own original experiment that is of great importance to the agricultural industry.
